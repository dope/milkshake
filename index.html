Welcome to Milkshake

Milkshake is Google Reader and Feedly merged into one and then simplified and then revamped from the ground up focusing on readability and you, the user.

About Milkshake

The main objective when creating Milkshake was to give the user a good experience and let them decide what to read and not force them into reading something that's not relevant to them. This isn't a discovery app, it's for reading.

Add Your Links

Adding your blogs URL's to Milkshake couldn't be easier, When using the pastebox modal, you can easily build a collection of your favourite articles and blogs.

Pick your favourites

Milkshake gives you the option of favouriting articles and blogs that you can keep coming back to again and again, extremely useful for reference guides and tutorials.

Read On The Go

Read anywhere, everywhere and anyhow. Kindle, iOS, Android, Blackberry, Mac & PC. Milkshake even saves your articles offline for them No-Wi-Fi moments.
